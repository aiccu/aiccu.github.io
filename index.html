FAQ

Why does AICCU need the Annual Member Survey? In short, it is critical to our state-level advocacy. Much of the advocacy work that is done on behalf of AICCU institutions focus on where state funding and independent institutions intersect (CalGrant, Community College Transfers). The private non-profit sector in California is diverse and complex. Responses from our members is critical to giving policymakers and other stakeholders an fuller story of the important work that is being done in the sector.

For example, legislative efforts to increase the CalGrant formula hinged on our survey collection in 2017. AICCU used this survey data to count aggregate Hispanic CalGrant students in the Independent sector, which played a key role in gaining the support of the Latino Caucus. This information is not collected by CSAC and is thus only available in aggregate via these surveys. Compare this to centralized collection that occurs in the CSU/UCs.
